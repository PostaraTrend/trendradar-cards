Our Story

Built from a decade of enterprise systems experience.

PostaraTrend was born from a simple observation: brands and agencies are always one step behind the trend curve — reacting to what has already peaked instead of predicting what is about to rise.

Our founding team spent over a decade building enterprise-grade technology systems at some of Canada's largest organisations. We took that same discipline — systematic, measurable, built to operate at scale — and applied it to a problem that affects every brand in the market: staying ahead of trends and turning them into content, fast.

PostaraTrend Technologies Inc. is incorporated in Alberta under the Business Corporations Act (Alberta) and headquartered in Calgary, with operations extending into the United States.

Questions

Frequently asked.

Everything you need to know about PostaraTrend and what we offer.

What can I use today?+
Our YYC Rental Studio product line is live now — a set of digital tools for Alberta landlords, available through our shop. Our autonomous publishing platform is in active development and open to a small group of early-access partners. Join the early access list below to be notified as we open new cohorts.
Who is the platform for?+
Brands, creators, agencies, and organisations that need to stay visible and relevant across social channels without building a full in-house content operation. If your team is always racing to catch up with trends, PostaraTrend is built for you.
How is this different from a social scheduler?+
Schedulers post what you give them. PostaraTrend detects emerging trends, generates brand-safe content around them, runs that content through automated quality checks, and publishes on a planned schedule — closing the loop from intelligence to output.
Will I have control over what gets published?+
Yes. You define your brand voice, approval rules, and publishing cadence. The platform can run in fully automated mode, human-in-the-loop review mode, or anywhere in between — depending on your governance requirements.
What does early access include?+
Priority onboarding when we open the next cohort, preferred pricing for founding customers, direct input into the product roadmap, and hands-on support from our team during your initial rollout.
Are you open to partnerships and investment?+
Yes — we are in conversation with agencies exploring white-label arrangements and with investors interested in the content intelligence category. Use the contact form below and we will set up a call.
